Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide customized corporate gifts and promotional products across UAE?

Yes. Notebook Advertising LLC supplies customized corporate gifts, promotional merchandise, exhibition giveaways, employee welcome kits, branded office products, and promotional items across Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, and all UAE regions.

What branding and printing methods do you provide?

We support screen printing, digital printing, UV printing, DTF printing, engraving, heat transfer, sticker labeling, packaging branding, and product mockup support depending on the selected item and order requirement.

Can you handle bulk corporate orders and exhibition giveaway requirements?

Yes. Our team manages bulk corporate gift orders, exhibition giveaways, event merchandise, branded packaging, delivery coordination, and repeat-order support for businesses across the UAE.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for customized products?

MOQ depends on the product, branding method, and availability. Some ready promotional items start from smaller quantities, while custom production, packaging, and imported merchandise may require higher quantities.

How long does production and delivery take?

Production timelines vary by product, quantity, branding method, and proof approval. Standard UAE orders are coordinated as quickly as possible after artwork confirmation, sample approval, and payment processing.

Do you provide product samples or branding mockups before production?

Yes. We can provide product samples, digital mockups, and branding previews where applicable so your team can confirm product choice, logo placement, and finishing details before bulk production.

What payment methods do you accept for corporate and bulk orders?

Payment options can include bank transfer, cash, cheque, or agreed corporate payment terms depending on the order value, client account status, and project timeline.
